📘 User Guide

Basic Interaction

  • Input: Click the numeric buttons (0-9) and operation buttons or use your keyboard to enter values.
  • Operations: Supports standard math Addition (+), Subtraction (−), Multiplication (×), and Division (÷).

Advanced Display & Logic

  • Dynamic View: Your full equation builds on the main screen in real-time. When you click = (or press Enter), the calculation history moves to the top line while the result stays below.
  • Active Operator: The selected math symbol (+, −, etc.) stays highlighted until the calculation is complete.

Corrections & Errors

  • Delete (⌫): Use the backspace button to remove the last character entered. This works for both numbers and math operators!
  • All Clear (AC): Use AC (or Escape) to reset the entire calculator.
  • Error Handling: The system detects invalid operations, such as dividing by zero, and displays an "Error" message.
